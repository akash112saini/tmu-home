Frequently Asked Questions

85% Outstanding Placement Rate One of the key indicators of a university's success is its placement records. TMU boasts an outstanding placement rate of 82% in the academic year 2022-23.

Highest Package Offered - 60 LPA The university's commitment to providing quality education is evident in the remarkable achievement of a 60 LPA package offered to TMU students, showcasing the recognition and demand for graduates from the university.

National and International Collaborations With over 100 collaborations with national and international organizations, TMU ensures that its students have access to diverse and enriching opportunities.

NIRF Innovation Category TMU secured a position in the 51-100 rank band in the NIRF innovation category, highlighting its commitment to fostering innovation in education.

Excellence in Skills Development Acknowledgement from Observe Now and LinkedIn Talent Solutions for Excellence in Skills Development further solidifies TMU's standing.

Outlook India Ranking TMU holds the 45th rank among the Top 50 State Private Universities by Outlook India, a testament to its overall excellence.

With over 28,000 alumni, TMU has created a robust network of successful individuals contributing to various fields globally.

TMU offers over 150+ career-oriented programmes globally.

14 on-campus colleges and independent departments for diverse learning.

Specialized courses in Medical, Dental, Engineering, and Management.

The university's lush green campus spanning over 130 acres provides a conducive environment for learning. State-of-the-art facilities, including sports complexes and libraries, contribute to a holistic educational experience.

Incubation Success and Recognition TMU's Business Incubation Centre's recognition by the UP state government further emphasizes TMU's success in fostering innovation & research.

Industry Collaborations TMU has established strong ties with leading industries, providing students with valuable insights and opportunities. Collaborations with IBM, AutoCAD, TCS-iON, and others enhance the practical exposure students receive.

Adherence to National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 TMU is strategically aligning with the future of education by implementing NEP 2020, adopting a national credit framework and redesigning the curriculum.

Outcome-Based Education (OBE) TMU's focus on OBE enhances the overall quality of learning, preparing students for the challenges of the future.

TMU stands committed to the ideals of Lord Mahaveer - Right Philosophy, Right Knowledge and Right Conduct in all spheres of activity and aspires to be the ultimate destination for world-class education.
